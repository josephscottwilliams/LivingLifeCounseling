Our Mission

Our focus is you. We want you to feel welcome and accepted from the moment you open our doors. We provide professional and affordable counseling for all ages and walks of life and we strive to enable you to face life's challenges so that you feel better than when you arrived.

Frequently Asked Questions

We accept: Amerigroup, Beacon, Blue Cross Blue Shield (BCBS), Cenpatico, Cigna, Medicaid, Tricare, and Superior. We also accept out of network payments.

You may pay via credit/debit card, check, or cash.

Give us a call for a 10-15 minute free consult. Schedule an appt. at that time. Needed information for insurance is Name, DOB, Member ID. Address and DOB of actual carrier of the insurance. First visit paperwork is listed on the website or can be provided in the lobby before their visit. First visit will last from 60-80 minutes. Visits for minors require a guardian to attend the first visit to meet at the beginning and ending of the appt. Following visits will be for child . Parents are welcome to voice concern or questions at anytime, preferably before a session begins. Following visits will be 50-60 minutes.

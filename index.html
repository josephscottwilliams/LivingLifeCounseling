Our Mission

Our mission is to offer quality counseling services with compassion, hope, and empowerment for all those who need change to reach their full potential. We provide professional and affordable counseling for all ages and walks of life! We understand the courage it takes to reach out for help and we strive our best to help you feel welcome and accepted while also preparing you with support, knowledge, and guidance to face life's challenges.

Live the life you envision for yourself!

At Living Life Counseling we value your trust and truly believe you are doing the best you can. Life happens, we can't always predict what comes next and even a small turn of events can lead to huge life changes. Lost your sense of purpose or maybe that happiness you thought you once had? Have no idea how to navigate the new and confusing circumstances you now find yourself in? We're here for you!

Become

"What lies behind you and what lies before you
is nothing compared to what lies within you"

Often get caught up in comparisons? Not feeling like you're good enough, or have low self esteem? This frequently can turn into anxiety and depression and keeps us from becoming what we set out to be. Let us help you find the confidence to BECOME who YOU want to be! You are unique and different, and your life has purpose. We believe everyone has value, is important, and we want to work with you to become your very best self.



Life is a Journey

Many times we get caught up in achieving goals we have for life and we forget to enjoy the journey. Relationships get tough, raising children may prove more difficult each day, maybe our parents are aging and have increasing health issues? Many of us get caught in the misconception that we are a product of others, get bogged down, or forget to focus on the many good and positive things around us. We believe everyone has the power to choose no matter what situations may occur. We can let life happen to us, or we can decide our path. You are not alone! We are here to help you through life's detours and see beyond to a brighter future.
